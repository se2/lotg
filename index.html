Love of the Game is a community outreach program aiming to integrate universities and people with physical and cognitive disabilities through sports activities.

As a community outreach program and aspiring non-profit, we extend the joys of sports to those who need it the most. Through integrating varsity collegiate athletes and surrounding communities on campus, we provide opportunities for inclusion for disabled and disadvantaged individuals. Our sporting events service various demographics channeling community service efforts from multiple angles.

This is an initiative built and run by life-long athletes who wish to pay forward the benefits that sports have given them. We believe everyone has the right to exercise, to be included, and to have fun! We do all that we can to provide every attendee the ability to connect, learn and grow!
