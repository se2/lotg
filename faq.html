FAQ’s


Why was LOTG founded?
LOTG was founded in the Spring of 2015 to enrich the lives of the disadvantaged community through sports from those who love it most.


Is LOTG a non-profit organization?
LOTG is an aspiring non-profit, hoping to be established and scalable in the few years. We are currently developing our program and incubating our organization within the University of Massachusetts – Lowell.


How much do LOTG events cost?
Our events are 100% free. From attendees to guardians, our events are made possible by our university and sponsors.


What sports opportunities do you offer?
We offer a variety of adaptable sports. Our programs are available to any skill/experience level. We’ve held basketball, softball, soccer, volleyball, and field hockey. We collaborate with student athletes and coaches to create sports clinics.


What disabilities/ages do you serve?
We serve individuals on the spectrum of developmental disabilities. Our age restriction is eight and above.


Where and how often are your events?
Our events are ran on college campuses during the academic year.
